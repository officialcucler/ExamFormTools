Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2024

1. Information We Collect

ExamFormTools is committed to protecting your privacy. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or uploaded files. All processing happens locally in your browser.

2. How We Use Information

Since we don't collect any data, we don't use or share any information. Your documents remain on your device and are processed entirely client-side.

3. Data Security

Your files are never uploaded to our servers. All image processing, resizing, and compression occurs in your web browser using JavaScript. No data leaves your device.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google AdSense for advertising. Google may collect information as described in their privacy policy. We do not share any user data with advertisers.

5. Cookies

We may use cookies for analytics and advertising purposes. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

6. GDPR Compliance

As a service that processes data locally, we comply with GDPR requirements. No personal data is collected or processed on our servers.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our contact page.
